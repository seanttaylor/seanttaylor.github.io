Who We Are

Brown Paper Bag is an expanding network of artists who seek new opportunities to collaborate across disciplines, to create audiences for new work and to provide a launchpad for promising artists to access key resources for career development.

We are determined to create as many opportunities as we can for artists to be seen, to be heard and to connect with one another. This commitment is rooted in the belief that we do our best work we work in community and collaboration with others, that the best support for creative people can only come from other creative people.

All art is interdisciplinary. All art is synthesis: of eye and ear, of thought and feeling, of mind and heart.

———

The BPB Editorial Team, Spring 2023

Submission Guidelines

We are thrilled that you're interested in sharing your work with us! Please adhere to the following guidelines when preparing your materials for submission.

Our Editorial Team is pleased to accept submissions in the following categories only.

  • Visual Art - Three to five images no less than 1024x768 in pixel dimensions
  • Poetry - Two to four poems per submission
  • Short Fiction - One to two pieces no more than 5500 words each
  • Feature - One piece no more than 7500 words
  • Excerpted Long Form - One piece no more than 4500 words

*** THE SUBMISSION DEADLINE IS June 1, 2023 ***

  • ALL written works must be rendered in 14pt Arial or Times New Roman typeface
  • DO NOT submit in more than one category
  • DO NOT include any identifying information on the submitted work itself, including name, pen name, dates, contact information such as email addresses or social media handles

Acceptance notices will be sent via email no earlier than July 31. Unfortunately we will not be able to provide declination notices regarding work we will not feature in the issue. Acceptance notices will include additional instructions for providing us any supplemental materials such as bios, headshots or image specifications for visual art submissions.

We expect to go to print no earlier than September 2023. Each contributor will receive a copy of the issue via USPS Priority Mail. The BPB team will contact featured contributors for mailing addresses and delivery instructions before printing. Additional copies of the magazine will be available for purchase.

Please direct any additional inquiries to info@bpbmagazine.com. We humbly ask your patience; we are a small team but we will do our best to offer a timely reply.
