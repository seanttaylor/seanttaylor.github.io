Our Solution

Core features of the Parcely Smart Shipping Platform

Transparency

Today's shipment tracking platforms only tell you where a package has been. Our IoT-enabled shipping boxes provide dozens of data points: from your package's up-to-the-moment location to ambient temperature and exposure to liquids.

Real-time data

The brains in our boxes—our smart shipping module—allows us to gather data from the field and push it to our mobile and web apps or your custom REST API service. Real-time data means real-time decisions.

Reliability

The transparency of a global logistics platform powered by real-time data and analytics means a more reliable solution for shippers and customers.

Sustainability

Parcely smart shipping boxes are designed to be reused, reducing the millions of metric tons of cardboard waste generated annually.

What is it?

Parcely creates reusable geolocation-enabled shipping boxes, allowing shippers and customers to track shipments in real-time. We have a propietary IoT device built into our boxes that captures and transmits telemetry data to our platform. Shippers and customers have access to their data through our apps; shippers can also integrate with our platform directly via REST API.

How does it work?

Merchants send their merchandise in our shipping boxes. Because the boxes are globally unique, we can track them anywhere within our platform. When the box reaches its destination, the recipient returns the postage-paid box to us. Rinse. Repeat. We keep thousands of our boxes in continuous circulation. This helps reduce the cardboard waste that is a growing environmental concern given the explosion of e-commerce and direct-to-consumer businesses. To customers who choose our Small Business or Enterprise plans, we regularly send fresh shipping boxes to keep businesses humming for the duration of the chosen plan. Our boxes are also available à la carte for a flat rate.

Who is it for?

Our boxes are designed with high-volume shipping use cases in mind but Parcely is also great solution for shipping high-value items as well, like luxury goods or collectibles. Our Smart Shipping Platform provides unmatched transparency and security as it ensures both shipper and customer know exactly where packages are at all times.
